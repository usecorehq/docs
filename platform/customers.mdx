title: "Customer Management"
description: "Build customer profiles, track history, and manage relationships"
---

The Customers module helps you build a database of your customers and track their interactions with your business. Customer profiles are created automatically from orders and bookings, or you can add them manually.

## Customer list

Navigate to **Customers** to view all your customer profiles. Search by name or phone number to quickly find a customer.

## Customer profiles

Each customer profile includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Name | Full name |
| Phone | Contact number |
| Email | Email address |
| Address | Physical address |
| Date of birth | Birthday |
| Gender | Gender |
| Tags | Labels like VIP, Regular, First Timer |
| Preferences | Notes about customer preferences |
| Marketing consent | Email and SMS opt-in flags |

## Customer stats

On each customer's detail page, you can see:

- **Total orders** — Number of orders placed
- **Total spent** — Lifetime spending
- **First order date** — When they first visited
- **Last order date** — Their most recent visit

## Order and booking history

The customer detail page shows complete transaction history:

- **Order history** — All orders with items, totals, and dates
- **Booking history** — All reservations and their statuses
- **Visit history** — Timestamps of every visit

## Tags and segmentation

Use tags to categorize customers:

- **VIP** — High-value customers
- **Regular** — Frequent visitors
- **First Timer** — New customers
- Custom tags you define

Filter the customer list by tags to create segments for targeted outreach.

## Customer dashboard stats

At the top of the Customers page, key metrics give you an overview:

- Total customers
- Active customers
- Total revenue from customers
- Average spend per customer
- Top customers by spending

## Auto-capture

Customer profiles are created automatically when:

- A new order is placed with customer details
- A booking is approved with guest information
- A public booking is submitted from the digital menu

This means your customer database grows organically with every transaction.

## Marketing

Navigate to **Customers > Marketing** for campaign tools:

- Bulk email campaigns
- SMS notifications
- Loyalty programs and rewards

### Loyalty program

Navigate to **Customers > Loyalty** to set up a points-based rewards system for your customers.

## Settings

Navigate to **Customers > Settings** to configure CRM preferences and default behaviors.

title: "Customer Management"
description: "Know your customers, remember their history, and keep them coming back"
---

Every time someone places an order or makes a booking, Qorelly can save their details. Over time, you build up a list of everyone who's visited your business  what they ordered, how much they've spent, and when they last came in. No extra effort required.

## Your customer list

Go to **Customers** to see everyone. You can search by name or phone number to quickly find who you're looking for.

## Customer profiles

Each customer has a profile with:

- **Name**, **phone**, and **email**
- **Address**
- **Date of birth**  great for birthday offers
- **Tags**  labels like VIP, Regular, or First Timer
- **Preferences**  any notes about what they like
- **Marketing preferences**  whether they've opted in to emails or SMS

## Seeing their history

Open any customer's profile and you can see everything:

- **Total orders**  How many times they've ordered
- **Total spent**  How much they've spent with you overall
- **First visit**  When they first came
- **Last visit**  When they were last here
- **Order history**  Every order, with what they had and how much it cost
- **Booking history**  All their reservations
- **Visit history**  A timeline of every visit

## Tags and groups

Tags help you organize your customers into groups:

- **VIP**  Your most valued customers
- **Regular**  The ones who keep coming back
- **First Timer**  Brand new customers
- **Custom tags**  Create your own, like "corporate" or "birthday club"

You can filter your entire customer list by tags, making it easy to find specific groups when you need them.

## Quick stats

At the top of the Customers page, you'll see a summary:

- Total number of customers
- How many are active
- Total revenue from all customers
- Average spend per customer
- Your top customers by spending

## Automatic customer capture

The best part? You don't have to manually add most of your customers. Qorelly creates profiles automatically when:

- Someone places an order with their details
- You approve a booking with guest information
- A customer books online from your digital menu

Your customer list grows naturally with every transaction.

## Marketing tools

Go to **Customers > Marketing** to reach out to your customers:

- Send email campaigns to groups of customers
- Send SMS messages
- Run loyalty programs with rewards

### Loyalty program

Go to **Customers > Loyalty** to set up a points-based rewards system. Customers earn points with purchases and can redeem them for rewards  a great way to keep people coming back.

## Settings

Go to **Customers > Settings** to adjust how customer management works for your business.

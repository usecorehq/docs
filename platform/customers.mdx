## Your customer list

Go to **Customers** to see everyone. At the top you'll see a summary:

| Stat | What it means |
|------|--------------|
| **Total customers** | Everyone in your database |
| **Active customers** | Customers who have placed at least one order |
| **Total revenue** | Combined spend from all customers |
| **Avg. spend** | Average spend per customer |

Use the filter tabs to narrow down who you're looking at:

- **All** — your full customer database
- **Active** — customers who have ordered before
- **New** — recently added customers
- **Top Spenders** — highest lifetime spend first — useful for identifying your most valuable regulars

Search by name, phone, or email to find a specific customer quickly.

## Adding a customer

### From the Customers page

Click **Add Customer** (top right).

<img
  src="/images/customers-add-PLACEHOLDER.png"
  alt="Add customer modal with name, phone, and email fields"
/>

Name is the only required field. Phone and email are optional but worth adding — phone is used for booking lookups and email is needed for marketing.

### From POS (most common)

You don't need to come to this page first. When taking an order in POS, tap **Link customer** in the order panel and select **New customer**. Fill in the details and they're added to your database instantly.

<Tip>
  Adding customers from POS is the fastest workflow for most businesses. Staff can capture customer details at the point of sale without interrupting the order flow.
</Tip>

## Customer profiles

Click the arrow on any customer row to open their full profile. You'll see:

- Name, phone, and email
- Total orders and total ₦ spent
- First visit and last visit dates
- Full order history — every order, what they had, and how they paid
- Booking history — all their reservations
- Tags and preferences

### Tags

Use tags to group customers: VIP, Regular, First Timer, or any custom tag you create. You can filter your full customer list by tag, which makes it easy to find specific groups when you need them.

---

## Exporting your customer data

Click **Export CSV** (top right of the customer list) to download your full customer database as a spreadsheet. Useful for importing into a marketing tool, or keeping an offline backup.

---

## Marketing

Go to **Customers > Marketing** to reach out to your customers by email or SMS. Send to your full list or filter by tags to target a specific group.

## Loyalty

Go to **Customers > Loyalty** to set up a points-based rewards system. Customers earn points with purchases and can redeem them for rewards — a straightforward way to bring people back.

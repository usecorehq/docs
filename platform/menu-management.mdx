This is where you set up everything your business sells or offers  whether that's dishes, services, products, or medicines (it depends on your [business type](/business-types)).

## Categories

Categories help you group your items so they're easy to find. Go to **Menu > Categories** to manage them.

### Creating a category

1. Click **Add Category**
2. Give it a name (like "Appetizers", "Main Course", or "Beverages")
3. You can also add subcategories if you want to get more specific
4. Set the order you want them to appear in

### Subcategories

You can organize things into layers. For example:
- **Beverages** (main category)
  - Hot Drinks
  - Cold Drinks
  - Smoothies

## Your items

Go to **Menu > Items** to see and manage everything you offer.

### Adding an item

Click **Add Item** and fill in what you need:

| Field | What it is | Required? |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Name | What you call it | Yes |
| Description | A short description for your customers | No |
| Price | How much it costs | Yes |
| Category | Which group it belongs to | Yes |
| Image | A photo to show customers | No |
| SKU | A code for tracking (helpful for retail) | No |
| Barcode | For scanning at checkout | No |

### Choose where items show up

Each item can appear in different parts of Qorelly. You decide:

- **Menu**  Visible on your public digital menu that customers can see
- **POS**  Available when your team is creating orders
- **Inventory**  Tracked as a stock item
- **Bookings**  Available as something customers can book

### Track your stock

If you want to keep an eye on how much of something you have left, turn on inventory tracking for that item. You can set:

- **Quantity**  How many you currently have
- **Reorder point**  When stock drops below this number, you'll get an alert
- **Supplier**  Who you order it from

### Turn items on and off

If something is temporarily unavailable, just toggle it off. It won't show up in the POS or on your digital menu, but it stays in the system so you can turn it back on whenever you're ready.

## Grid view or list view

You can switch between two ways to look at your items:

- **Grid view**  Shows items as cards with images, great for browsing
- **List view**  A compact table, great for managing lots of items quickly

Qorelly remembers which view you prefer.

## Import and export

Got a big menu? You don't have to add items one by one.

1. Click **Import/Export** in the toolbar
2. **Export** downloads everything as a spreadsheet (CSV file)
3. **Import** lets you upload a spreadsheet to add or update items in bulk

<Tip>
  Export your menu first to see the right format, then edit the spreadsheet and re-import it. Much faster than doing it all by hand.
</Tip>

## QR code

Want customers to see your menu on their phones? Generate a QR code:

1. Go to **Menu > QR Code**
2. Download the QR code image
3. Print it and put it on your tables, door, receipts, or anywhere else

Customers just scan it with their phone camera and your full menu pops up  no app needed. See [Digital Menu](/platform/digital-menu) for more on how this works.

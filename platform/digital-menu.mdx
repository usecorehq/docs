---

Qorelly creates a public menu page for your business that anyone can open on their phone, tablet, or computer — no download needed.

## Your menu link

Your digital menu lives at a link like:

```
https://qorelly.com/menu/your-business-name
```

The link is based on your business name and you can customize it in **Settings > General**. Share it on social media, put it in your bio, or text it to customers.

## What your customers see

When someone opens your menu, they'll see:

- **Your branding** — Your logo, business name, address, and phone number
- **Categories** — They can browse by category (like "Starters", "Mains", "Drinks")
- **All your items** — Each one with its name, description, price, and photo
- **Search** — They can search for something specific
- **Item details** — Tap any item for a closer look

### How it looks

You can choose from four layout styles for your menu:

| Layout | What it looks like |
|--------|-------------------|
| Grid | Items shown as cards in a grid, with photos |
| Portrait | Tall cards with big images |
| Horizontal | Image on one side, details on the other |
| Text-only | Clean and simple, no images |

You can even set different layouts for phones and desktops, and choose how many columns the grid uses. Configure all of this in **Settings > Appearance**.

## My List — customer favorites

When you turn this on, customers can save their favorite items to a personal list right on the menu page.

- They don't need to create an account or log in
- Their favorites are saved on their device
- They can share their list with friends via a link

Turn this on in **Settings > General** under **My List**.

## QR code

The easiest way to get customers to your menu is a QR code:

1. Go to **Menu > QR Code**
2. Download the image
3. Print it out and place it on tables, at the entrance, on receipts, on flyers — anywhere

Customers just point their phone camera at it and your menu opens instantly.

## Let customers book from the menu

If you've turned on [public booking](/platform/bookings#public-booking), your menu page will also show a **Book a Table** button. Customers can make a reservation right from the same page — no phone call needed.

## Your brand, your look

The digital menu automatically uses your brand settings:

- **Your colors** — The theme color you set in **Settings > Appearance**
- **Your logo** — Displayed at the top of the page
- **Your layout** — The style you chose

When someone shares your menu link on WhatsApp, Facebook, or Twitter, it shows a nice preview with your business name and description.

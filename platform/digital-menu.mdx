---

Qorelly generates a public digital menu page for your business that customers can access from any device — no app download required.

## Public menu page

Your digital menu is available at:

```
https://qorelly.com/menu/your-business-slug
```

The slug is automatically generated from your business name and can be customized in **Settings > General**.

## What customers see

The public menu page displays:

- **Business info** — Logo, name, address, and phone number
- **Category navigation** — Browse by category (horizontal tabs or expandable sections)
- **Menu items** — Each item shows its name, description, price, and image
- **Search** — Customers can search for items by name
- **Item details** — Tap any item to see a full description, price, and image

### Layout options

Your digital menu can be displayed in four different layouts:

| Layout | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Grid | Card-based grid with images (configurable columns) |
| Portrait | Vertical cards with prominent images |
| Horizontal | Side-by-side image and text |
| Text-only | Minimal design without images |

Configure the layout in **Settings > Appearance**. You can set different layouts for desktop and mobile views, and configure the number of grid columns.

## My List (customer favorites)

When enabled, customers can save their favorite items to a personal "My List" on the menu page.

- Favorites are stored locally on the customer's device
- Customers can share their list via URL
- No account or login required

Enable this feature in **Settings > General** by toggling **My List**.

## QR code

Generate a QR code that links directly to your digital menu:

1. Go to **Menu > QR Code**
2. Your QR code is displayed and ready to download
3. Print it for table cards, window stickers, receipts, or marketing materials

Customers scan the QR code with their phone camera to instantly view your menu.

## Public booking from the menu

If [public booking](/platform/bookings#public-booking) is enabled, customers see a **Book a Table** button on your menu page. They can make a reservation directly without calling or visiting.

## Customization

The digital menu page inherits your brand settings:

- **Theme color** — Your primary brand color from **Settings > Appearance**
- **Logo** — Your business logo
- **Layout** — The menu layout you selected

The page also includes Open Graph meta tags for social media sharing, so your menu looks great when shared on platforms like WhatsApp, Facebook, or Twitter.

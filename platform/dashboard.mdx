The Dashboard is the first thing you see when you log in. It gives you a quick snapshot of how your day is going and helps you jump into any part of Qorelly.

## Overview

The Overview section shows you the numbers that matter most:

- **Today's Orders** — How many orders you've had so far today
- **Today's Revenue** — How much money has come in from completed orders
- **This Week's Orders** — A running total for the week

### Revenue chart

There's an hourly chart that shows how your sales are spread across the day (from 6 AM to 11 PM). You can:

- **Look at different days** — Use the arrows to go back and forward
- **Compare** — See how today stacks up against yesterday or the same day last week

### Multiple locations?

If you have more than one branch, just pick a location from the top of the screen. Everything on the dashboard updates to show numbers for that specific branch.

## Apps

The Apps section gives you quick shortcuts to the features you've turned on:

- **Menu** — Jump into your menu items and categories
- **POS** — Go straight to your orders
- **Inventory** — Check your stock levels
- **Bookings** — See your reservations

Only the features you've enabled in **Settings > Apps** show up here.

## Getting around

The sidebar on the left is your main menu. From here you can get to everything:

| Section | What it's for |
|---------|--------------|
| Dashboard | Your daily overview and stats |
| Menu | Managing your items and categories |
| POS | Taking and tracking orders |
| Inventory | Keeping tabs on your stock |
| Bookings | Reservations, tables, and rooms |
| Customers | Your customer list and profiles |
| Team | Your staff, roles, and activity |
| Settings | Everything about your business setup |

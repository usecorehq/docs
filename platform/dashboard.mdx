The Dashboard is your home screen after logging in. It provides a real-time overview of your business activity and quick access to all enabled features.

## Overview tab

The Overview tab displays key metrics for the current day:

- **Today's Orders** — Total number of orders placed today
- **Today's Revenue** — Total revenue from completed orders
- **This Week's Orders** — Running total for the current week

### Revenue chart

An hourly revenue chart (6 AM to 11 PM) shows your sales distribution throughout the day. You can:

- **Navigate dates** — Use the previous/next arrows to view different days
- **Compare periods** — Toggle comparison mode to see the delta against the prior day or prior week

### Multi-location filtering

If your business has multiple locations, select a location from the top bar to filter all dashboard data to that specific branch.

## Apps tab

The Apps tab provides quick-access cards to all your enabled modules:

- **Menu** — Manage your menu items and categories
- **POS** — Access the point-of-sale system
- **Inventory** — View stock levels and alerts
- **Bookings** — Manage reservations

Only apps that are enabled in your **Settings > Apps** configuration will appear here.

## Navigation

The sidebar navigation gives you access to all platform features:

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Dashboard | Overview and metrics |
| Menu | Categories and menu items |
| POS | Orders, items, and history |
| Inventory | Stock, suppliers, and purchase orders |
| Bookings | Calendar, tables, rooms, and staff |
| Customers | Customer profiles and CRM |
| Team | Team members, roles, and activity |
| Settings | Business configuration |

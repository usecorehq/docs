Go to **Settings** from the sidebar to adjust how your Qorelly account works.

## General

**Settings > General** — The basics about your business:

| Setting | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| Business name | The name that shows everywhere |
| Slug | The short name used in your digital menu link |
| Logo | Your business logo |
| Phone | Your contact number |
| Address | Your physical address |
| Public status | Whether your business shows up in public listings |
| Public booking | Let customers book online from your menu page |
| My List | Let customers save favorite items on your menu page |

## Appearance

**Settings > Appearance** — Make Qorelly feel like your own:

- **Theme color** — Pick your brand color. It shows across the dashboard and your digital menu
- **Menu layout (desktop)** — How your digital menu looks on computers: Grid, Portrait, Horizontal, or Text-only
- **Menu layout (mobile)** — How it looks on phones — you can set this separately
- **Grid columns** — How many columns the grid layout uses

## Business type

**Settings > Business** — Switch your [business type](/business-types) anytime. This updates the language and defaults across the platform to match how your business actually works.

## Integrations

**Settings > Integrations** — Connect other services you use:

- **Payments** — Accept payments through Stripe, Paystack, or other providers
- **Email** — Send order confirmations, booking notifications, and marketing emails
- **SMS** — Text customers with updates and promotions
- **Analytics** — Track how people use your menu and platform

## Danger zone

**Settings > Danger Zone** — Things you can't undo:

- **Reset data** — Wipe all your orders, bookings, and customer data
- **Delete business** — Permanently remove your business and everything in it

<Warning>
  These actions can't be reversed. If you have data you want to keep, make sure to export it first.
</Warning>

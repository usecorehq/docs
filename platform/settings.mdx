---

Navigate to **Settings** from the sidebar to configure all aspects of your Qorelly account.

## General settings

**Settings > General** — Configure your core business information:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Business name | Your business display name |
| Slug | URL-friendly identifier for your digital menu |
| Logo | Upload your business logo |
| Phone | Business contact number |
| Address | Physical business address |
| Public status | Whether your business appears in public listings |
| Public booking | Allow customers to book directly from your menu |
| My List | Enable the customer favorites feature on your digital menu |

## Appearance

**Settings > Appearance** — Customize how your platform and digital menu look:

- **Theme color** — Choose your primary brand color, applied across the dashboard and digital menu
- **Menu layout (desktop)** — Grid, Portrait, Horizontal, or Text-only
- **Menu layout (mobile)** — Separate layout option for mobile devices
- **Grid columns** — Number of columns for grid layout (desktop and mobile)

## Business type

**Settings > Business** — Change your [business type](/business-types) to update terminology and default configurations across the platform.

## Integrations

**Settings > Integrations** — Connect third-party services:

- **Payment processing** — Stripe, Paystack, and other providers
- **Email** — SendGrid, Mailgun for transactional and marketing emails
- **SMS** — Twilio, AWS SNS for notifications
- **Analytics** — Google Analytics, Mixpanel for tracking

## Danger zone

**Settings > Danger Zone** — Destructive actions that cannot be undone:

- **Reset data** — Clear all orders, bookings, and customer data
- **Delete business** — Permanently delete your business and all associated data

<Warning>
  Actions in the Danger Zone are irreversible. Make sure to export any data you need before proceeding.
</Warning>

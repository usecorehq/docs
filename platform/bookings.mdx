Whether it's a dinner reservation, a spa appointment, or a consultation, Qorelly makes it easy to manage bookings. You get a calendar view, table and room management, staff assignment, and even the option to let customers book directly from your digital menu.

## Calendar views

Open **Bookings** and you'll see your reservations laid out in a calendar. You can switch between three views:

- **Month** — A bird's-eye view of the whole month
- **Week** — Hourly slots from 7 AM to 10 PM, great for spotting gaps
- **Day** — A detailed look at a single day

Click on any booking to see the details or make changes.

## What's in a booking

Each booking captures:

| | |
|-------|-------------|
| **Customer name** | Who made the reservation |
| **Phone** | Their contact number |
| **Date & time** | When they're coming |
| **Party size** | How many people |
| **Table or room** | Where they'll be seated or served |
| **Status** | Where the booking stands |
| **Notes** | Any special requests ("birthday dinner", "window seat", etc.) |

## How bookings flow

Every booking goes through these stages:

```
Pending → Confirmed → Arrived → Completed
                                     ↘ Cancelled
```

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| Pending | A new booking waiting for you to confirm |
| Confirmed | You've approved it — the customer is expected |
| Arrived | They're here |
| Completed | All done |
| Cancelled | The booking was called off |

### Approving bookings

When a new booking comes in, you can approve or decline it with one click right from the dashboard. When you approve a booking, Qorelly automatically creates or updates the customer's profile — so your customer list grows naturally.

## Tables

Go to **Bookings > Tables** to set up your tables.

For each table, you can:
- Give it a name or number
- Set how many people it seats
- Link it to a room or area
- Turn it on or off

## Floor plan

Go to **Bookings > Floor Plan** to arrange your tables visually, just like your actual layout. Drag tables around to match your space.

<Info>
  The floor plan works especially well on tablets and iPads — your staff can quickly see which tables are free and tap to take action.
</Info>

## Rooms

Go to **Bookings > Rooms** to manage your rooms. These are separate from tables and work for:

- Private dining areas in restaurants
- Treatment rooms in spas
- Consultation rooms in pharmacies or clinics

## Staff assignment

Go to **Bookings > Staff** to assign specific team members to bookings. This way, everyone knows who's responsible for which customer.

## Let customers book online

You can let customers make their own bookings directly from your [digital menu](/platform/digital-menu) page.

To turn it on:
1. Go to **Settings > General**
2. Switch on **Public Booking**

Once it's on, your digital menu shows a **Book a Table** button. Customers can pick a date and time, enter their name and phone number, say how many people, and add any notes.

These bookings come in as **Pending** — you still get to approve or decline them before they're confirmed.

<Tip>
  Every booking that comes in — whether from your team or from a customer — helps build your customer list automatically. No extra work needed.
</Tip>

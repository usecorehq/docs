Whether you have 2 staff or 200, Qorelly makes it easy to get everyone set up, give them the right access, and keep track of what's happening.

## Your team

Go to **Team** to see everyone who's part of your business. For each person you can see:

- Their name and email
- What role they have (Owner, Manager, Cashier, etc.)
- Their status — whether they've been invited, are active, disabled, or have left
- When they last logged in

## Inviting someone new

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    On the Team page, click **Invite Member**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email and pick a role">
    Type in their email address and choose what role they should have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They get an email">
    An invitation lands in their inbox. They just need to click the link to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They're in">
    Once they accept, they show up as **active** on your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If someone hasn't accepted yet, you can always resend the invitation.

## Roles

Roles control what each person can see and do. Qorelly comes with these built-in:

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| Owner | Everything — this is whoever created the business |
| Manager | Run day-to-day operations, manage the team, handle most settings |
| Cashier | Take orders and process payments |
| Waiter | View and create orders, manage tables |
| Chef | See incoming orders and update their status as they're prepared |

<Info>
  The role names change depending on your [business type](/business-types). Spas get "Therapist" and "Stylist", pharmacies get "Pharmacist", and so on.
</Info>

### Create your own roles

Need something more specific? You can create custom roles:

1. Go to the **Roles** section on the Team page
2. Click **Create Role**
3. Name it and pick exactly which permissions it should have

See [Roles & Permissions](/administration/roles-permissions) for the full breakdown of what you can control.

## Permissions at a glance

Each role gets specific permissions for each part of Qorelly:

| Area | What you can control |
|------|---------------------|
| Dashboard | Who can see the overview |
| Menu | Who can view, edit, or delete items |
| POS | Who can see, create, or edit orders |
| Inventory | Who can view or make changes |
| Bookings | Who can see, create, edit, or cancel bookings |
| Customers | Who can view, edit, or delete customers |
| Reports | Who can view or download reports |
| Settings | Who can see or change settings |
| Team | Who can view the team, invite people, or manage members |

## Activity log

The Team page keeps a log of what everyone's been doing — logins, order actions, and other key events. It's all timestamped, so you always have a clear record.

## Managing your team

For each team member, you can:

- **Change their role** — Move them to a different role anytime
- **Set a PIN** — Give them a PIN code for [terminal mode](/administration/terminal-mode) access
- **Disable** — Temporarily pause their access without removing them
- **Remove** — Take them off the team permanently

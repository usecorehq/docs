The POS is where you and your team take orders and manage them from start to finish. Whether it's dine-in, takeaway, or delivery, everything happens here.

## Your POS dashboard

When you open the POS, you see all your active orders at a glance. You can filter them by:

- **All Active**  Everything that's currently in progress
- **Pending**  New orders waiting to be started
- **Preparing**  Orders being worked on right now
- **Ready**  Done and waiting for the customer

The dashboard refreshes automatically, so you're always seeing the latest. Each order shows the table or room, the customer's name, the total, and where it's at in the process.

At the top, you'll see today's quick stats  how many orders you've had, how much revenue, and how many are still pending or being prepared.

## Taking a new order

<Note>
  Your team members need the right permissions to create orders. See [Roles & Permissions](/administration/roles-permissions) for how to set this up.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new order">
    Click **New Order** from the POS dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the order type">
    Is this **Dine-in**, **Takeaway**, or **Delivery**?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a table (for dine-in)">
    If the customer is eating in, pick their table from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add items">
    Browse your menu or search for what the customer wants. For each item, you can change the quantity and add any special notes (like "no onions" or "extra spicy").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link a customer (optional)">
    You can attach the order to an existing customer, or quickly create a new profile right there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Review everything and submit. The order is now in the system and your team can start working on it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How orders move through the system

Every order follows a natural flow:

```
Pending → Preparing → Ready → Completed
                                    ↘ Cancelled
```

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| Pending | Just placed  waiting for your team to pick it up |
| Preparing | Your team is working on it |
| Ready | It's done and waiting for the customer |
| Completed | Delivered and finished |
| Cancelled | The order was called off (you'll need to confirm this) |

<Info>
  These labels change depending on your [business type](/business-types). For example, a spa shows "Being worked on" instead of "Preparing"  because that makes more sense.
</Info>

## Payments

Each order tracks whether it's been paid:

- **Pending**  No payment yet
- **Paid**  Fully paid
- **Partially paid**  Some of the amount has been collected

Qorelly supports cash, card, and QR-based payments.

## Order history

Want to look back at past orders? Go to **POS > History**. You can filter by date, order status, or payment status. Click on any order to see the full details  what was ordered, who the customer was, how they paid, and when everything happened.

## Stock alerts

If you're tracking inventory, the POS works hand-in-hand with your stock levels:

- **Low stock alerts**  Go to **POS > Alerts** to see which items are running low
- **Automatic updates**  When an order is completed, your stock numbers update automatically

## Managing your POS items

Go to **POS > Items** to see what's available for ordering. From here you can:

- Check stock levels at a glance
- Turn items on or off
- Set up SKU codes and barcodes for faster checkout

Qorelly is a comprehensive business management platform designed for restaurants, spas, pharmacies, retail stores, fuel stations, and other service-based businesses. It provides everything you need to manage your operations — from digital menus and point-of-sale to inventory tracking, bookings, customer management, and team collaboration.

## Why Qorelly?

- **Multi-business support** — One platform that adapts to restaurants, spas, pharmacies, retail, hospitality, and fuel stations
- **Web + Apps** — Full-featured web dashboard plus a Flutter app for iOS, Android, tablets, iPads, and desktop — all with offline support
- **All-in-one** — POS, inventory, bookings, CRM, and team management in a single platform
- **Multi-location** — Manage multiple branches from one account
- **Role-based access** — Granular permissions for owners, managers, cashiers, waiters, chefs, and custom roles

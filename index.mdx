Qorelly helps you run your business from one place. Whether you own a restaurant, spa, pharmacy, retail store, fuel station, or any service-based business, Qorelly brings everything together — your menu, orders, stock, bookings, customers, and team.

## Why Qorelly?

- **Works for any business** — One platform that speaks the language of restaurants, spas, pharmacies, retail, hospitality, and fuel stations
- **Use it anywhere** — A web dashboard you can open in any browser, plus an app for your phone, tablet, iPad, or desktop computer
- **Everything in one place** — Take orders, track stock, manage bookings, keep up with customers, and run your team — without switching between apps
- **Multiple branches? No problem** — Manage all your locations from a single account
- **Your team, your rules** — Decide exactly who can see and do what with flexible roles and permissions

title: "Offline Mode"
description: "Continue taking orders even without internet connectivity"
---

QorellyMobile is built with an offline-first architecture, so your business operations don't stop when the internet does. Orders created offline are stored locally and automatically synced when connectivity is restored.

## How it works

The app continuously monitors your network connectivity by checking the connection to the Qorelly servers. When the connection drops:

1. An **offline banner** appears at the top of the screen
2. You can continue creating orders normally
3. Orders are saved to the device's local SQLite database
4. When connectivity returns, orders sync automatically in the background

## What works offline

### Order creation

You can create complete orders while offline, including:

- Selecting order type (dine-in, takeaway, delivery)
- Adding menu items with quantities
- Adding special instructions
- Processing payments (cash)
- Completing orders

### Cached data

The app caches essential data locally so it's available offline:

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Menu items | All your menu items with names, prices, and images |
| Categories | Menu categories for browsing |
| Restaurant info | Business details and settings |

This cached data is refreshed automatically whenever the app is online.

## Automatic sync

When the device reconnects to the internet:

1. The app detects the connectivity change
2. All unsynced orders are uploaded to the server
3. Each order's items and payments are synced
4. Successfully synced orders are marked as complete
5. Menu items and categories are refreshed from the server

If a sync fails for any order, the error is recorded and the app retries on the next connectivity event. No data is lost.

## Offline indicators

The app provides clear visual feedback about connectivity:

- **Online** — No indicator, normal operation
- **Offline** — Banner displayed, orders saved locally
- **Syncing** — Brief indicator while uploading queued orders

## Best practices

<Tip>
  Open the app while connected to the internet before going to a location with poor connectivity. This ensures your menu items, categories, and restaurant data are cached and ready for offline use.
</Tip>

- Keep the app updated to get the latest offline improvements
- Orders created offline will appear on the web dashboard once synced
- Cash payments work offline; card payments require connectivity
- The app stores offline orders until they are successfully synced — there is no expiration

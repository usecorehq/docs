QorellyMobile offers six distinct operational modes that change the app's navigation, visible features, and default screen. These modes work across all platforms — phones, tablets, iPads, and desktop. Each mode is designed for a specific type of business or workflow.

## Selecting a mode

On your first login, the app recommends a mode based on your [business type](/business-types):

| Business Type | Recommended Mode |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Restaurant | Standard |
| Fast food / Cafe | Quick Service |
| Spa / Salon | Bookings |
| Retail | Retail |
| Pharmacy | Retail |
| Fuel Station | Quick Service |

You can accept the recommendation or choose any mode. To change your mode later, go to **More > Mode**.

## Available modes

### Standard

The default mode for full-service restaurants and general businesses. Provides access to all features.

**Bottom tabs:** Sale, Inventory, Orders, Notifications, More

Best for businesses that use POS, inventory, and bookings together.

### Quick Service

Streamlined for fast counters, cafes, and takeaway businesses where speed is the priority.

**Bottom tabs:** Sale, Orders, Notifications, More

**Hidden features:** Inventory, Floor Plan, Bookings

Best for businesses focused on quick order processing without table management.

### POS

A dedicated fullscreen point-of-sale mode for maximum efficiency at the counter.

**Bottom tabs:** None — fullscreen POS interface

**Hidden features:** Inventory, Floor Plan, Bookings, Customers, Team, Reports, Work Periods

Best for dedicated POS terminals where the device is used only for taking orders.

### Full Service

Designed for restaurants with table service and floor plan management.

**Bottom tabs:** Sale, Inventory, Orders, Notifications, More

All features are available, with the floor plan prominently accessible on tablets.

Best for sit-down restaurants where table assignment and floor plans are important.

### Retail

Optimized for shops and retail stores with a focus on inventory and sales.

**Bottom tabs:** Sale, Inventory, Orders, Customers, More

**Hidden features:** Floor Plan, Bookings

Best for retail stores, pharmacies, and shops that need inventory tracking with POS.

### Bookings

Designed for appointment-based businesses like spas, salons, and clinics.

**Bottom tabs:** Bookings, Checkout, Customers, More

**Hidden features:** Sale, Inventory, Floor Plan

Best for businesses where scheduling appointments is the primary workflow.

## Switching modes

Your mode selection is saved to your profile and synced across devices. To switch:

1. Go to **More** from the bottom tab bar
2. Tap your current mode card at the top
3. Select a new mode
4. The app immediately updates its navigation and available features

<Info>
  Switching modes does not delete any data. All your orders, bookings, inventory, and customer data is preserved regardless of which mode you use.
</Info>

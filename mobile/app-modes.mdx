Not every business uses Qorelly the same way. A fast food counter doesn't need the same layout as a spa, and a retail shop has different priorities than a sit-down restaurant. That's why the app comes with six different modes — each one changes what you see and how the app is laid out.

Modes work on every device: phones, tablets, iPads, and desktop.

## Picking your mode

The first time you log in, Qorelly suggests a mode based on your [business type](/business-types):

| Your business | Suggested mode |
|---------------|---------------|
| Restaurant | Standard |
| Fast food / Cafe | Quick Service |
| Spa / Salon | Bookings |
| Retail store | Retail |
| Pharmacy | Retail |
| Fuel Station | Quick Service |

You can go with the suggestion or pick any mode you like. To change it later, go to **More > Mode**.

## The six modes

### Standard

Everything is available. This is the all-rounder for businesses that use a bit of everything.

**Tabs you see:** Sale, Inventory, Orders, Notifications, More

### Quick Service

Stripped down for speed. Perfect for counters, cafes, and takeaway spots where you just need to take orders fast.

**Tabs you see:** Sale, Orders, Notifications, More

**What's hidden:** Inventory, Floor Plan, Bookings

### POS

A dedicated full-screen checkout experience. Nothing else on screen — just the POS.

**Tabs you see:** None — the whole screen is the POS

**What's hidden:** Everything except taking and completing orders

### Full Service

Built for sit-down restaurants where tables and floor plans matter.

**Tabs you see:** Sale, Inventory, Orders, Notifications, More

All features are available, with easy access to the floor plan on tablets and iPads.

### Retail

Focused on selling products and managing stock. Great for shops, pharmacies, and stores.

**Tabs you see:** Sale, Inventory, Orders, Customers, More

**What's hidden:** Floor Plan, Bookings

### Bookings

Built for appointment-based businesses — spas, salons, clinics, and consultancies.

**Tabs you see:** Bookings, Checkout, Customers, More

**What's hidden:** Sale, Inventory, Floor Plan

## Switching modes

You can change your mode anytime:

1. Go to **More** from the bottom tab bar
2. Tap the mode card at the top
3. Pick a new mode
4. The app updates immediately

<Info>
  Switching modes doesn't delete anything. All your orders, bookings, stock, and customer data stays right where it is. You're just changing the view, not the data.
</Info>

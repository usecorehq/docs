Qorelly isn't just a website. There's also an app you can install on pretty much any device — your iPhone, Android phone, iPad, Android tablet, or even your Mac or Windows computer. It gives you the same features as the web dashboard, designed for whatever screen you're using.

## Where you can use it

| Platform | Devices |
|----------|---------|
| iOS | iPhone, iPad, iPad Pro, iPad Mini |
| Android | Phones and tablets from Samsung, Lenovo, and others |
| macOS | MacBook, iMac, Mac Mini |
| Windows | PCs, laptops, Surface devices |
| Linux | Desktops and laptops |

Want the full breakdown? See [Supported Platforms](/mobile/supported-platforms).

## What makes the app special

- **Works on any device** — Phone, tablet, iPad, desktop — pick what suits you
- **Works offline** — No internet? No problem. Keep taking orders and everything syncs up when you're back online
- **Print receipts** — Connect a Bluetooth printer or use built-in POS hardware
- **Scan barcodes** — Use your phone or tablet camera to scan products at checkout
- **6 modes** — Choose a layout that matches your workflow
- **Multiple branches** — Switch between locations easily

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    - **iPhone or iPad** — Download from the App Store
    - **Android phone or tablet** — Download from the Google Play Store
    - **Mac, Windows, or Linux** — Download from [qorelly.com](https://qorelly.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Use the same email and password you use on the web. Everything is connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your mode">
    The first time you log in, Qorelly suggests a [mode](/mobile/app-modes) based on your business type. You can go with the suggestion or pick a different one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're ready">
    The app opens to the screen that matters most for your business — usually the POS if you're taking orders, or the bookings calendar if you run on appointments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Everything stays in sync

Whether you're on the web, on your phone, or on a tablet at the counter — it's all the same account and the same data. An order placed on your iPad shows up on the web dashboard. A customer added on the website shows up in the app. Everything is always up to date.

## Getting around the app

On phones and tablets, you'll see a tab bar at the bottom of the screen. What tabs you see depends on your [app mode](/mobile/app-modes).

On desktop, you get a sidebar on the left — similar to the web dashboard.

The **More** menu takes you to additional sections:

- Menu Management
- Team
- Reports
- Settings
- Work Periods
- Locations
- Help & Support

## The app adapts to your screen

The layout changes depending on the size of your screen:

**On a phone** — Everything is in a single column, easy to use with one hand. Navigation is at the bottom.

**On a tablet or iPad** — You get multi-column grids, side-by-side views, and a bigger calendar. Great for counters and front desks.

**On desktop** — Full sidebar navigation, multi-panel views, and more space for data tables and charts. Ideal for managers and back-office work.

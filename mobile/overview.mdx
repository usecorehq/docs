QorellyMobile is the cross-platform companion to the Qorelly web dashboard. It runs natively on **iOS**, **Android**, **tablets and iPads**, and **desktop** (macOS, Windows, Linux)  all from a single codebase.

## Where you can use it

| Platform | Devices | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| iOS | iPhone, iPad, iPad Pro | Available |
| Android | Phones, tablets | Available |
| macOS | MacBook, iMac, Mac Mini | Available |
| Windows | Desktop PCs, laptops | Available |
| Linux | Desktop PCs, laptops | Available |

Want the full breakdown? See [Supported Platforms](/mobile/supported-platforms).

## What makes the app special

- **Works on any device**  Phone, tablet, iPad, desktop  pick what suits you
- **Works offline**  No internet? No problem. Keep taking orders and everything syncs up when you're back online
- **Print receipts**  Connect a Bluetooth printer or use built-in POS hardware
- **Scan barcodes**  Use your phone or tablet camera to scan products at checkout
- **6 modes**  Choose a layout that matches your workflow
- **Multiple branches**  Switch between locations easily

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    - **iPhone or iPad**  Download from the App Store
    - **Android phone or tablet**  Download from the Google Play Store
    - **Mac, Windows, or Linux**  Download from [qorelly.com](https://qorelly.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Use the same email and password you use on the web. Everything is connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your mode">
    The first time you log in, Qorelly suggests a [mode](/mobile/app-modes) based on your business type. You can go with the suggestion or pick a different one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're ready">
    The app opens to the screen that matters most for your business  usually the POS if you're taking orders, or the bookings calendar if you run on appointments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The **More** menu takes you to additional sections:

- Menu Management
- Team
- Reports
- Settings
- Work Periods
- Locations
- Help & Support

## Adaptive layouts by screen size

The app automatically adjusts its layout based on screen size:

| Screen size | Device examples | Layout |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Small (\< 600px) | iPhones, Android phones | Single-column, bottom tab bar |
| Medium (600px–1100px) | iPads, Android tablets | Multi-column grids, split views, enhanced calendar |
| Large (\> 1100px) | Desktop, iPad Pro landscape | Full multi-panel layout, sidebar navigation |

### Phone layout

- Single-column interface optimized for one-handed use
- Bottom tab bar for primary navigation
- Swipe gestures for common actions

### Tablet and iPad layout

- Multi-column grids for menu items (4–5 columns)
- Split-view screens for order management
- Larger floor plan view for table management
- Enhanced bookings calendar with more time slots visible
- Side-by-side order creation and item browsing

### Desktop layout

- Full sidebar navigation
- Multi-panel views with maximum information density
- Keyboard shortcuts for power users
- Larger data tables and charts

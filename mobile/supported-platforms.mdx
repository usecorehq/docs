- **Floor plan**  The bigger screen makes the table layout easy to see and manage

## Which device is right for you?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Restaurant counter or cashier" icon="cash-register">
    **Go with an Android tablet or iPad.**

    Mount it at the counter for a big, easy-to-use touchscreen. Use **POS mode** or **Quick Service mode** to keep things simple and fast.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Table-side ordering" icon="utensils">
    **Go with an iPad Mini or Android phone.**

    Small enough to carry around, but powerful enough to take full orders. Use **Full Service mode** to see the floor plan and assign tables.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Spa or salon front desk" icon="spa">
    **Go with an iPad or Android tablet.**

    Perfect for checking customers in and managing the day's appointments. Use **Bookings mode** to focus on scheduling.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Back office or management" icon="desktop">
    **Go with a Mac or Windows computer.**

    Best for reviewing reports, managing inventory, and configuring settings. The desktop layout gives you the most room to work.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="On the go" icon="motorcycle">
    **Go with your phone (iPhone or Android).**

    Check in on your business from anywhere. Offline mode means you can even take orders at pop-up events or places with spotty internet.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

The mobile sale flow is built for one thing: getting from "the customer wants this" to "paid and done" as fast as possible. It works the same on phone, tablet, iPad, and desktop — the layout just stretches to fit the screen.

## Building an order

Open the **Sale** tab. You'll see your menu as a grid of cards, with category pills along the top.

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Filter by category** | Tap a category pill at the top |
| **Search** | Tap the search bar — it works across names, SKUs, and barcodes |
| **Add an item** | Tap the item card. A green border appears with quantity controls |
| **Change quantity** | Use the **+** and **−** buttons on the selected item |

On a phone, a **View Cart** pill appears at the bottom as soon as you add the first item. Tap it to open the full cart. On a tablet, the cart lives in a side panel on the right and is always visible.

### Working offline

If you lose your internet connection, an orange banner appears: *"You're offline — orders will sync when connection returns."* Keep taking orders as normal. Everything saves on the device and uploads automatically the moment you're back online.

## The cart

Tapping **View Cart** opens the full checkout screen.

<img
  src="/images/mobile-cart.png"
  alt="Cart screen with items, order type, customer, payment, and summary"
/>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the items">
    Each line shows the quantity, name, modifiers, and any note. Tap a row to change the quantity in a popup. Tap the bin icon to remove the line.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the order type">
    Pick **Dine-in**, **Takeaway**, or **Delivery**. For dine-in, a **Table** picker appears below — tap to pick from your floor plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link a customer (optional)">
    Search for an existing customer, or type their details to create one on the spot. Leave blank for a walk-in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose payment">
    Pick **Cash**, **Card (POS)**, or **Bank Transfer**. For cash, an **Amount tendered** field appears with a live change calculator — it shows ₦ change due or warns "Insufficient amount" in red.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply a discount">
    Enter a discount in the summary section. Subtotal, VAT, and total update instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish the order">
    Tap the big button at the bottom — labelled with the total (e.g. **"Create Order · ₦5,200"**). Or use **Save draft** to hold the order, or **Pay later** to send it through without collecting yet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When the order is placed, you'll see a green tick, the order number, and quick buttons to **View Order**, **Print receipt** (if a printer is connected), and start a **New Order**.

## Scan-to-checkout

Customers browsing your digital menu can build their own cart, then show their phone at the till for a fast ring-up. Tap the **QR** button on the Sale screen and scan the customer's cart code.

A confirmation sheet shows their order:

- **Yellow warning chips** if anything's changed — *"X items no longer available"* or *"X items with updated prices"*
- **Updated price notes** under any item whose price has changed since they scanned (*"was ₦X when scanned"*)
- **Skipped items** at the bottom if anything in their cart is no longer available

Tap **Add to order** to drop the verified items into your cart, then continue checkout as normal. Tap **Cancel** to back out.

<Tip>
  Scan-to-checkout is the fastest way to ring up a customer who already knows what they want. It also cuts down on misheard orders — what's on their phone is what goes in the cart.
</Tip>

## Splitting a bill

Tap **Split bill** on any order to divide the total between people. Two modes:

### Equal split

Set how many people are paying (2 to 10). The screen shows the per-person amount in large branded type. For each person, tap **Charge person N** to take their share. As each one pays, a green tick replaces the button.

### By item

Set how many people are paying. Each line item has chips numbered 1, 2, 3… — tap a chip to assign that item to a person. Under the items, each person's running total updates as you assign. Tap **Charge person N** when you're ready to take their share.

After tapping a charge button, a **Payment method** sheet appears — pick **Cash**, **Card**, or **Transfer** for that person and confirm.

<Info>
  Split bill works alongside the rest of the checkout — the order is still one order in your reports, just paid by multiple people.
</Info>

## Adding items to an existing order

Need to bring something extra to a table that's already ordered? Open the order, tap **Add items**, and you're back in the menu grid. Tap items to add them, then tap **Confirm · ₦[subtotal]** to merge them into the original order. The kitchen sees the new items and the bill updates.

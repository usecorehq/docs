---
title: "Receipt Printing"
description: "Print receipts from your phone or tablet"
---

Qorelly lets you print receipts directly from the app on your phone or tablet. You can use a Bluetooth thermal printer or, if your device has a built-in printer, it works with that too.

<Note>
  Receipt printing works on iOS and Android devices (phones and tablets). It's not available on desktop at this time.
</Note>

## Using a Bluetooth printer

This is the most common setup. You connect a small wireless thermal printer (the kind that uses receipt paper rolls) via Bluetooth. It works with standard 58mm and 80mm paper widths.

### How to set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn on your printer">
    Power it on and make sure it's in pairing mode (usually a blinking light means it's ready).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pair it with your device">
    Go to your phone or tablet's Bluetooth settings and connect to the printer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select it in Qorelly">
    The app automatically sees your paired Bluetooth printers. Just pick yours from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it">
    Print a test receipt to make sure everything looks good.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Devices with built-in printers

Some Android POS devices (like Sunyard terminals) come with a receipt printer built right in. If you're using one of these, Qorelly connects to the printer directly  no Bluetooth pairing needed. It's faster and simpler.

## What's on a receipt

Every printed receipt includes:

- Your **business logo** (if you've uploaded one)
- **Business name** and contact details
- **Order number** and the date and time
- **Each item** with its quantity and price
- **Subtotal**, any discounts, and the **total**
- **Payment method**  cash, card, etc.
- **Who served them**  the staff member who processed the order

## Printing a receipt

After completing an order:

1. Open the order details
2. Tap **Print**
3. The receipt prints on your connected printer

Need to print it again later? You can reprint any receipt from the order history.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The printer doesn't show up">
    Make sure it's powered on and paired in your device's Bluetooth settings. Try turning the printer off and on again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The print quality is bad">
    Check you're using the right paper size (58mm or 80mm) for your printer. If the paper is old or has been stored in heat, it may not print well  try a fresh roll.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Printing is slow">
    Bluetooth speed depends on how far away the printer is. Move your device closer and make sure nothing is blocking the signal.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

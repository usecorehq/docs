---
title: "Receipt Printing"
description: "Print receipts via Bluetooth thermal printers or native POS hardware"
---

QorellyMobile supports receipt printing on phones and tablets through two methods: Bluetooth thermal printers and native POS hardware integration.

<Note>
  Receipt printing is available on iOS and Android devices (phones and tablets). Desktop platforms do not currently support direct receipt printing.
</Note>

## Bluetooth thermal printers

The most common setup for small businesses. Works with standard 58mm and 80mm Bluetooth thermal printers.

### Setting up a Bluetooth printer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn on the printer">
    Power on your Bluetooth thermal printer and ensure it is in pairing mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pair with your device">
    Go to your phone or tablet's Bluetooth settings and pair with the printer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect in QorellyMobile">
    The app automatically detects paired Bluetooth printers. Select your printer from the available devices list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test print">
    Print a test receipt to verify the connection and print quality.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Native POS hardware

For businesses using dedicated POS terminals (such as Sunyard devices), the app integrates directly with the built-in printer hardware.

Native POS printing:
- Uses the device's built-in thermal printer
- No Bluetooth pairing required
- Faster print speed
- Supports the device's specific paper width

## Receipt contents

Printed receipts include:

- **Business logo** (if configured)
- **Business name** and contact details
- **Order number** and date/time
- **Order items** with quantities and prices
- **Subtotal, discounts, and total**
- **Payment method**
- **Staff member** who processed the order

## Printing from orders

You can print a receipt from any completed order:

1. Open the order detail screen
2. Tap the **Print** button
3. The receipt prints on the connected printer

You can also reprint receipts at any time from the order history.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Printer not found">
    Ensure the printer is powered on and paired in your device's Bluetooth settings. Restart the printer and try again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Print quality is poor">
    Check that you are using the correct paper size for your printer (58mm or 80mm). Replace the thermal paper roll if it is old or damaged.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Printing is slow">
    Bluetooth printing speed depends on the connection quality. Move closer to the printer and ensure there are no obstructions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Head to [qorelly.com](https://qorelly.com) and click **Sign Up**. Enter your name, email, and choose a password.

    You'll get a verification email — just click the link to confirm your account, and you're ready to log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your business">
    Once you log in for the first time, Qorelly will walk you through setting up your business:

    - **Business name** — What's your restaurant, spa, or store called?
    - **Business type** — Pick the one that fits: Restaurant, Spa/Salon, Pharmacy, Retail, Hospitality, or Fuel Station
    - **Phone number** and **Address**
    - **Logo** — Upload your logo if you have one (you can always add it later)

    The type you choose shapes how Qorelly looks and feels — the wording, the features it highlights, and the defaults it sets up for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on the features you need">
    Go to **Settings > Apps** and choose what you want to use:

    - **Menu** — Set up a digital menu your customers can see
    - **POS** — Start taking and managing orders
    - **Inventory** — Keep track of what you have in stock
    - **Bookings** — Accept reservations and appointments

    You need at least one turned on, but you can always add or remove them later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your menu">
    Go to **Menu > Categories** and create your first category (like "Main Course" or "Haircuts"). Then start adding items with names, prices, descriptions, and photos.

    These items will show up everywhere — in your POS when you're taking orders, on your digital menu for customers, and in your inventory if you're tracking stock.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Go to **Team** and send invitations to your staff by email. Give each person a role — like Manager, Cashier, Waiter, or Chef — so they only see what's relevant to them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [qorelly.com](https://qorelly.com) and click **Sign Up**. Enter your full name, email address, and a password.

    You will receive a verification email — click the link to confirm your account before logging in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your business">
    After your first login, you will be prompted to create your business profile:

    - **Business name** — The name of your restaurant, spa, or store
    - **Business type** — Choose from Restaurant, Spa/Salon, Pharmacy, Retail, Hospitality, or Fuel Station
    - **Phone number** and **Address**
    - **Logo** — Upload your business logo (optional)

    Your business type determines the default terminology and available features throughout the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable your apps">
    Navigate to **Settings > Apps** to enable the modules you need:

    - **Menu** — Digital menu management
    - **POS** — Point of sale and order processing
    - **Inventory** — Stock tracking and purchase orders
    - **Bookings** — Reservations and table management

    At least one app must be enabled at all times.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your menu">
    Go to **Menu > Categories** to create your first category, then add menu items with names, descriptions, prices, and images.

    Your menu items will appear across all enabled apps — POS, digital menu, and inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Go to **Team** and invite team members by email. Assign roles like Manager, Cashier, Waiter, or Chef to control what each person can access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

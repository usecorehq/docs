Got more than one branch? Whether it's a chain of restaurants, a spa with multiple outlets, or a retail store with several locations — Qorelly lets you manage them all from a single account.

## Who this is for

- A restaurant with branches in different neighborhoods
- A spa with outlets in different parts of town
- A retail chain with stores across several cities

All your locations live under one Qorelly account. You can see everything together or drill into one specific branch.

## Setting up locations

Head to [Locations](/platform/locations) for step-by-step instructions on adding and managing your branches.

## How it works across the platform

### Dashboard

Pick a location from the top of the screen and everything updates:

- Revenue and order numbers show that branch's data
- Charts and comparisons are specific to that location
- Switching is instant — just pick a different branch

### POS

- New orders are automatically created under whichever location you've selected
- Order history can be filtered by branch
- Daily stats show numbers for the active location

### Inventory

- Stock is tracked separately at each location
- The same item can have different quantities at different branches
- Low stock alerts are specific to each location
- Purchase orders are tied to a location

### Bookings

- Tables and rooms belong to specific locations
- Bookings are filtered by the active branch
- Each location has its own floor plan

### Customers

Here's where it's different — **customer profiles are shared** across all your locations. If the same customer visits two of your branches, they have one profile with combined history. You get the full picture of their relationship with your business.

### Team

Team members can be assigned to specific locations. Their role and permissions apply at whatever location they're assigned to.

## What's shared vs. what's separate

| Shared across all locations | Separate at each location |
|----------------------------|--------------------------|
| Menu items and categories | Orders and revenue |
| Customer profiles | Bookings and reservations |
| Team member accounts | Stock levels |
| Business settings | Tables and rooms |
| Roles and permissions | Floor plan layout |

## Tips

- **Make your busiest branch the default** — It loads first when you and your team log in
- **Use clear names** — "Victoria Island Branch" is better than "Branch 1"
- **Compare locations** — Check reports for different branches to spot trends and opportunities
- **Assign your team** — Make sure each location has the right people in place

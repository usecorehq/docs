---
title: "Workspaces"
description: "Switch between multiple businesses or team memberships"
---

Workspaces let users who belong to multiple businesses switch between them without logging out. If you are a team member at more than one restaurant, spa, or store on Qorelly, each one appears as a separate workspace.

## How workspaces work

When you log in, Qorelly checks which businesses you are associated with:

- **As an owner** — Your own business appears as a workspace
- **As a team member** — Any business that has invited you appears as a workspace

If you belong to only one business, the app automatically selects it and takes you to the dashboard. If you belong to multiple, you see the workspace selector.

## Workspace selector

The workspace selector appears at `/workspaces` and shows:

- Business name and logo for each workspace
- Your role at each business
- Active/inactive status

Tap a workspace to enter it. All data in the dashboard, POS, inventory, and other modules is scoped to the selected workspace.

## Switching workspaces

To switch to a different workspace during a session:

1. Access the workspace selector from the navigation
2. Choose the business you want to work with
3. The dashboard refreshes with data from the selected workspace

Your active workspace is remembered and persisted between sessions, so you return to the same workspace when you next open the app.

## How data is scoped

Each workspace is completely isolated:

| Data | Scoped per workspace |
|------|---------------------|
| Orders | Yes |
| Menu items | Yes |
| Inventory | Yes |
| Bookings | Yes |
| Customers | Yes |
| Team members | Yes |
| Settings | Yes |
| Locations | Yes |

There is no data sharing between workspaces. A customer at Restaurant A is a separate record from a customer at Restaurant B, even if they share the same phone number.

## Admin impersonation

Super administrators can impersonate any workspace for debugging and support purposes. This is an internal feature that allows the admin to view and manage a business as if they were the owner, without needing the owner's credentials.
